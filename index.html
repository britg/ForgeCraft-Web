About. Learn about KIHOMAC, the stability behind FileRelay.

Since our founding in 2003, KIHOMAC has established an outstanding track record of support for the requirements of our US Government customers, as evidenced by our portfolio of “Exceptional” rated Contractor Performance Assessment Reporting System reports. Now, with nearly 100 people, we provide program management and system engineering services, conduct engineering analysis, and develop sustainment solutions for aerospace vehicles.

KIHOMAC is a veteran owned SBA certified SDB & 8(a) company with a projected graduation in 2014. However, we operate with a mature business practice. We have a rigorous accounting system that is Defense Contract Audit Agency (DCAA) audited and approved, and we submit annual provisional billing rates and incurred cost to DCAA for audit and approval. Our business results, in the form of financial statements, are independently audited by an outside accounting firm.
